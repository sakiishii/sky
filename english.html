Flat white

It's the most famous espresso drink in oceania.
A flat white is an Australian coffee drink made with espressoand steamed milk.Likely developed sometime around the 1960sAt the shop,we are particular about steam,which is the most important factorin making Flat white.The difference compared to a latte is that there's no frothed milk on top.With a flat white,you can enjoy the taste of the coffee more compared to a latte.Pleas try drinking a Flat white!
